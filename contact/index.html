❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are the games really free?

Yes! All games on GameVolt are 100% free to play. No ads, no paywalls, no subscriptions — just free games.

Do I need to create an account?

No account needed! Just visit and play instantly. Optional free account unlocks cloud saves, global leaderboards, and trophies across all games.

Can I play on mobile?

Absolutely! All our games are optimized for mobile devices with touch controls. Works great on phones and tablets.

My high score disappeared. What happened?

Local high scores are stored in your browser's local storage. Clearing browser data or using private/incognito mode will reset them. Leaderboard scores are saved permanently.

Can I suggest a new game?

We love suggestions! Send your game ideas to contact@gamevolt.io and we'll consider them for future development.

Response Time: We typically respond to emails within 24-48 hours. For urgent matters, please include "URGENT" in your subject line.

⚡ About GameVolt

GameVolt is an independent game studio from Sweden. We make original, free-to-play browser games with no advertisements. All our games are built from scratch using modern web technology and work on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop. No downloads, no account required — just click and play.
