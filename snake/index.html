Play Snake Online – Free Classic Snake Game

Relive the golden age of mobile gaming with Snake Neo – a free browser snake game inspired by the iconic Nokia phone classic. Guide your snake, eat food, grow longer and try not to crash. Simple rules, endless fun, and zero downloads needed.

Three Unique Game Modes

Nokia 3310 Mode – Pixel-perfect retro gameplay that looks and feels just like the original Nokia snake from the late 90s. Monochrome graphics, chunky pixels, and that satisfying classic speed. Pure nostalgia.

Neo Mode – A modern take on classic snake with synthwave aesthetics, neon glow effects and a pulsing electronic soundtrack. Progress through levels as the speed and challenge increase.

16-bit Endless Mode – Colorful retro-styled endless snake gameplay with wraparound mechanics. The snake exits one side of the screen and enters from the other – a fun twist that changes your strategy completely.

How to Play

Use arrow keys or swipe on mobile to change direction. Eat the food to grow your snake and increase your score. Avoid hitting the walls (in classic modes) or your own tail. The longer your snake gets, the harder it becomes. Works on desktop, tablet and phone.
