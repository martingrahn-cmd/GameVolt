Gridburn — Light-Cycle Duels in a Neon Arena

Gridburn is a free browser game in the classic light-cycle lineage that Tron made famous: your cycle rides forward relentlessly, burning a solid wall of light into the grid behind you. Box your rival in, make them crash first, and be the last one riding. The whole game leans into a neon synthwave arcade feel — glowing cyan-versus-orange trails, a dark grid arena, CRT scanlines and a striped retro sun on the menu screen.

Online multiplayer with a room code

Pick PLAY ONLINE, create a room and share the 4-character code — or just send the invite link, which drops your friend straight into the room with one tap. You each ride from your own phone, tablet or computer in real time; both games stay perfectly in sync. Best of 5 rounds, rematch in the same room, no account and no app needed.

Three AI opponents that think differently

Easy never plans ahead and happily boxes itself in. Medium plays for open space and survives long. Hard plays territory — it doesn't just survive, it actively cuts you off. There's also local 2-player on one keyboard (or two gamepads) and a solo practice mode where you chase your longest survival time on the global leaderboard.

Controls

WASD or arrow keys, touch (tap left / right to turn) or a gamepad — D-pad or stick steers and navigates, A selects, B goes back, Start pauses. First to 3 rounds takes the match. 31 trophies to earn, from your first crash to the Neon Legend platinum.

Gridburn FAQ

Can I play Gridburn online with a friend?

Yes. Pick PLAY ONLINE, create a room and share the 4-character code — or just send the invite link, which drops your friend straight into the room. You each ride from your own phone, tablet or computer in real time. No account and no app needed.

Is Gridburn free to play?

Yes — 100% free with no ads, no download and no signup. Signing in (free) is only for syncing your 31 trophies and putting your solo survival time on the global leaderboard.

How do you play Gridburn?

Your light cycle rides forward constantly and leaves a solid glowing trail. Steer with WASD, arrow keys, touch or a gamepad. If you hit a wall, a trail or your rival head-on, you crash — force your opponent to crash first. First to 3 rounds takes the match.

Does Gridburn have an AI opponent?

Yes, three difficulties. Easy never plans ahead and boxes itself in. Medium plays for open space. Hard plays territory — it doesn't just survive, it actively cuts you off.

Does Gridburn work with a gamepad?

Yes. The D-pad or left stick steers and navigates the menus, A selects, B goes back and Start pauses. In local 2-player a second gamepad steers player two.

Is Gridburn like Tron?

It's in the classic light-cycle lineage that Tron made famous — glowing trails, a dark grid arena and a neon synthwave look — built as an original free browser game with online multiplayer and AI opponents.
