    Chain Reaction — Free 2048 Puzzle Game Online

    Play Chain Reaction free in your browser — a neon-drenched take on the classic 2048 number puzzle with one electric twist: merges don't stop at one. When a merge lines up a new matching pair, the board cascades automatically, chaining merge after merge while a combo multiplier pumps up your score. Slide, merge, reach the 2048 tile, and keep going in endless mode. No download, no signup — just pure merge-puzzle strategy.

    How to Play Chain Reaction

    The rules take seconds to learn. On a 4×4 grid, every move slides all tiles in one direction at once. Two tiles with the same number slam together into a single tile worth double — 2 and 2 make 4, 4 and 4 make 8, all the way up to 2048 and beyond.

    • Swipe (mobile) or use the arrow keys / WASD (desktop) to slide tiles.
    • Match equal tiles to merge them into double the value.
    • Made a mistake? Undo rewinds your last move — once per game in Normal, three times in Easy mode.
    • The run ends when the board fills up and no merges are left. Reach 2048 first and you win — then continue in endless mode.

    The Chain Reaction Twist vs Classic 2048

    In classic 2048, one swipe means one round of merges. Here, if a merge creates a fresh matching pair in the same direction, the board keeps sliding on its own — a chain reaction. Every extra cascade step multiplies the points it scores: step two pays double, step three pays triple, and so on. Setting up long chains is the heart of the strategy — a well-planned swipe can rack up more points than a dozen ordinary moves. Classic wisdom still applies: keep your biggest tile locked in a corner and build toward it.

    Chain Reaction FAQ

    Is Chain Reaction really free to play?

    Yes. 100% free in your browser, no download and no signup needed. Sign in only if you want your trophies synced and a spot on the global leaderboard.

    How is Chain Reaction different from classic 2048?

    In classic 2048 each swipe resolves once. In Chain Reaction, when a merge creates a new matching pair, the board automatically slides again in the same direction — and each extra cascade step multiplies the points it earns (x2, x3, x4...). Long chains are where the big scores come from.

    What happens after I reach the 2048 tile?

    You win the run — and you can keep playing in endless mode, building even bigger tiles and pushing your high score and longest chain further.

    Can I undo a move?

    Yes. Normal difficulty gives you one undo per game, so spend it wisely. Easy mode grants three undos and also spawns fewer 4-tiles.

    Does it work on phones and tablets?

    Yes. Swipe on the board to slide tiles, with haptic feedback where supported. Your run auto-saves, so you can resume any time on the same device.

    There are 31 trophies in four tiers (15 bronze, 10 silver, 5 gold, 1 platinum), earned for tiles, chains, scores and more. High scores are kept locally, and signing in to GameVolt submits your best to the global leaderboard.
