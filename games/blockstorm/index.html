How to Play BlockStorm

BlockStorm is a classic falling-block puzzle game with a vibrant synthwave aesthetic. Pieces fall from the top of the screen — rotate and position them to complete horizontal lines. When a line is full, it clears and everything above drops down. The game ends when blocks stack to the top.

Master techniques like T-spins, back-to-back clears, and combos to rack up massive scores. Use the hold feature to save a piece for later and plan your moves using the next-piece preview.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is BlockStorm free to play?
Yes, completely free. No ads, no in-app purchases, no downloads. Just open the page and start stacking.
Can I play on my phone or tablet?
Yes. BlockStorm has full touch controls — swipe to move, tap to rotate, swipe up for hard drop. It works on any device with a modern browser.
What are the three game modes?
Marathon is an endurance challenge where speed increases over time. Sprint challenges you to clear 40 lines as fast as possible. Ultra gives you a fixed time limit to maximize your score.
What are the keyboard controls?
Arrow keys to move and rotate, Space for hard drop, Shift to hold a piece, and Down to soft drop. On mobile, use swipe gestures and taps.
How do I get on the leaderboard?
Create a free GameVolt account (just an email, no password needed). Your scores are then submitted to the global leaderboard automatically after each game. Each mode has its own separate ranking.
