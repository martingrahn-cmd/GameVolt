How to Play Axeluga

Axeluga is a classic vertical-scrolling space shooter (shmup) where you pilot a fighter ship through hostile space. Enemies approach in waves from above — shoot them down before they reach you, dodge their bullets, and collect power-ups dropped by defeated foes.

The game supports three input methods: keyboard (arrow keys + Z to shoot), touch controls (virtual joystick and fire button), and gamepad (any standard controller). Pick whatever feels most comfortable and dive into the action.

The Five Worlds

  • ☉
    World 1 — Asteroid Belt
    Navigate through drifting rocks and light enemy patrols. A warm-up that teaches the basics.
  • 🌈
    World 2 — Nebula Depths
    Reduced visibility and faster enemies. New bullet patterns demand sharper reflexes.
  • 🔨
    World 3 — Station Ruins
    Destroyed space stations create tight corridors. Armored enemies absorb more hits.
  • 👾
    World 4 — Alien Homeworld
    Organic enemy ships with unpredictable movement. Dense bullet curtains test your dodging.
  • ⭐
    World 5 — The Core
    The final gauntlet. Relentless waves, the toughest boss, and glory for those who survive.

Power-ups and Weapons

Defeated enemies occasionally drop power-ups. Grab weapon upgrades to increase your firepower from a single shot to a devastating spread. Shields give you an extra hit of protection. Bombs clear the screen of all enemies and bullets — save them for when things get overwhelming. Speed boosts let you weave through dense bullet patterns with precision.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Axeluga free to play?
Yes, completely free. No ads, no in-app purchases, no downloads. Just open the page and start shooting.
Can I play on my phone or tablet?
Absolutely. Axeluga has a virtual joystick and fire button designed for touch screens. It works on any device with a modern browser.
Does it support gamepads?
Yes. Connect any standard gamepad (Xbox, PlayStation, or compatible) and it will be detected automatically. You can also play with keyboard or touch controls.
How many levels are there?
Axeluga has 5 worlds, each with multiple stages and a unique boss battle at the end. The difficulty ramps up with new enemy types and bullet patterns in every world.
How difficult is the game?
It starts accessible — the first world teaches you the mechanics gently. Difficulty increases gradually, with later worlds demanding sharp reflexes and pattern recognition. Power-ups help balance the challenge.
