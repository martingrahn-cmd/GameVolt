How to Play Connect 4

Connect 4 is a two-player strategy game played on a vertical 7-column, 6-row grid. Players take turns dropping colored discs into columns. The disc falls to the lowest available position in that column. The first player to get four of their discs in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — wins the game.

It sounds simple, but the strategy runs deep. Block your opponent while building your own connections. Control the center columns for maximum flexibility. Set up "traps" where you have two possible ways to win on a single move — forcing your opponent into an impossible choice.

Strategy Tips

The center column is the most powerful position on the board. Discs placed there participate in the most possible four-in-a-row combinations, so try to claim the center early. Watch for diagonal threats — they're the hardest to spot and the most common way to lose.

When you have three in a row with an open space on both ends, you've created an unblockable win. Learning to set up these "double threats" is the key to beating stronger opponents and climbing the leaderboard.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Connect 4 free to play?
Yes, completely free. No ads, no in-app purchases, no downloads. Just open the page and start dropping discs.
Can I play on my phone or tablet?
Absolutely. Connect 4 is built mobile-first with touch support. Tap any column to drop your disc. It works on any device with a modern browser.
How difficult is the AI?
There are three levels. Easy is perfect for casual play. Medium provides a genuine challenge. Hard uses advanced lookahead strategy and is very tough to beat — only the sharpest players can outsmart it consistently.
How do I play 2-player on the same device?
Select "Player vs Player" from the menu. Both players take turns tapping columns on the same screen. It's perfect for sitting next to a friend or family member.
What trophies can I earn?
There are 31 trophies: 15 bronze, 10 silver, 5 gold, and 1 platinum. Earn them by winning games, beating the AI on each difficulty, building win streaks, and mastering advanced plays. Sign in to sync trophies across devices.
