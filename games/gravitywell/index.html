How to Play Gravity Well

You are a gravity well — a singularity drifting through deep space. Move across the screen to attract nearby cosmic particles into your gravitational pull. The closer particles get, the stronger the pull becomes, until they spiral in and are absorbed for points.

But the cosmos isn't all friendly. Antimatter particles appear in every wave, and touching even one will collapse your gravity well instantly. The key is reading particle trajectories, positioning yourself carefully, and knowing when to pull in and when to steer clear.

The Five Waves

  • 💫
    Wave 1 — Stellar Drift
    Gentle particles and slow antimatter. Learn the controls and build your first combos.
  • 🌠
    Wave 2 — Nebula Surge
    Faster particles and new cosmic debris. Timing your pulls becomes critical.
  • 💥
    Wave 3 — Asteroid Belt
    Dense particle fields with antimatter weaving through. Precision movement is key.
  • 🪐
    Wave 4 — Quantum Storm
    Particles change behavior mid-flight. Expect the unexpected in this chaotic wave.
  • 🌈
    Wave 5 — Event Horizon
    The ultimate challenge. Maximum speed, maximum antimatter, maximum glory.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Gravity Well free to play?
Yes, completely free. No ads, no in-app purchases, no downloads. Just open the page and play.
Can I play on my phone or tablet?
Absolutely. Gravity Well is designed with full touch support. Drag your finger to move the gravity well on mobile, or use your mouse on desktop.
How many waves are there?
There are 5 waves, each more intense than the last. New particle types, faster speeds, and more antimatter appear with every wave you survive.
Does Gravity Well support touch controls?
Yes. On mobile and tablet, drag your finger to move the gravity well. On desktop, use the mouse or keyboard. Controls feel responsive on any device.
How does scoring work?
You earn points by absorbing cosmic particles — each type has a different value. Absorbing particles in quick succession builds a combo multiplier for higher scores. Hitting antimatter ends your run immediately.
