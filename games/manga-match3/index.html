How to Play Manga Match

Swap two adjacent tiles to create a line of 3 or more matching symbols. Matched tiles disappear, new tiles cascade in from above, and chain reactions can trigger massive combos. Each level has a specific goal — clear a target number of tiles, reach a score threshold, or break through obstacles — all before running out of moves.

Match 4 tiles in a row to create a line blast power-up. Form an L-shape or T-shape to create an area bomb. Match 5 in a row for the devastating color nuke, which wipes every tile of one type from the board. Combine two power-ups for even bigger explosions.

Fever Mode

Every combo you chain fills the fever meter at the top of the screen. When it's full, Fever Mode activates — for a limited time, every match triggers an amplified cascade, multiplying your score and clearing the board at breakneck speed. Time your biggest combos to coincide with Fever Mode for explosive results.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Manga Match free to play?
Yes, completely free. No ads, no in-app purchases, no downloads. Just open the page and start matching.
Can I play on my phone or tablet?
Absolutely. Manga Match is built mobile-first with smooth swipe controls. It plays beautifully on phones, tablets, and desktops.
How many levels are there?
There are 80+ levels spread across 3 themed worlds, with more being added over time. Plus a new daily challenge puzzle every day.

Match 4 in a row for a line blast, form an L or T shape for an area bomb, and match 5 for a color nuke. You can also combine two power-ups by swapping them into each other for even more devastating effects.
What are daily challenges?
Every day at midnight UTC, a new challenge puzzle appears for all players. Complete it and submit your score to the daily leaderboard. Play every day to build your streak and earn streak-related trophies.
