Snake Neo update: 31 trophies and global high scores

Snake Neo was one of the last GameVolt games without trophies and cloud leaderboards. Not anymore — the classic just joined the full GameVolt experience.

Snake Neo is our take on the classic snake game in three distinct visual modes: pixel-perfect Nokia 3310 nostalgia, a glowing synthwave Neo mode, and a colorful 16-bit Fruit Chain twist. With this update it gets the same progression layer as the rest of the catalog:

What's new

  • 31 unlockable trophies — 15 bronze, 10 silver, 5 gold and 1 platinum, with achievement toasts popping right in the game as you earn them.
  • Local + global high scores — your personal bests are always tracked on your device, and signing in (free) puts your scores on the global leaderboard.
  • Cloud sync — trophies and high scores follow your account across phone, tablet and desktop.

As always, everything works without an account too — your progress is saved locally and syncs up the first time you sign in. No download, no ads, instant play.
