July 16, 2026 · Update

Signing in is now simpler — Google or a quick email code

We rebuilt how you sign in to GameVolt. There are no passwords to remember, it takes a couple of seconds, and it works the same whether you're on your phone, a tablet or a desktop.

Two ways in, no password

Open the sign-in box on any page and you'll see two options:

  • Sign in with Google — one tap and you're in.
  • Email — type your address and we send you a one-time code. Enter the code to sign in. The same email also contains a magic link, so on a computer you can just tap that instead.

That's it. No passwords to set, forget or reset.

Your progress follows you

Signing in is optional — every game still works as a guest, with your progress saved on your device. But when you do sign in, your trophies, high scores and saves sync to your account, so they follow you across phone, tablet and desktop. Start a run on the couch, pick it up at your desk.

Works when you install GameVolt too

If you've added GameVolt to your home screen, the email code is the reliable way in — you enter it right inside the app and stay put, no bouncing out to the browser. That was the main reason we added codes alongside the magic link.
