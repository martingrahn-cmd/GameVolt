Breakout: Neon Drift — the brick breaker with a twist

Our Breakout got a full overhaul. Neon Drift keeps the timeless Arkanoid formula but wraps it in a glowing synthwave-arcade look and adds one signature move that changes how you play — the Perfect Drift.

The twist: Perfect Drift

Plain Breakout is all about angles. Neon Drift adds control on top of them. Hit the ball with the edge of the paddle while you're sliding it fast in the same direction and you pull off a Perfect Drift — a skill-shot that curves and aims your shot. It's a risk-and-reward layer that lets you steer the ball into tight brick clusters, save a ball that was heading for the gutter, and set up big combos. Easy to bounce, satisfying to master.

Power-ups, a final boss & 31 trophies

Bricks drop power-ups as you climb — multiball, lasers, a wider paddle and extra lives — and the level progression builds up to a neon final boss to cap off a run. There are 31 trophies to chase across bronze, silver, gold and platinum tiers, laid out in a clean tiered grid so you can see exactly what's left to earn.

Synthwave soundtrack & global leaderboard

A synthwave soundtrack plays as you go, with a one-tap toggle to switch it on or off any time. Sign in (free) and your trophies and best score sync across phone, tablet and desktop and land you a spot on the global leaderboard. It plays with touch, mouse and keyboard — nothing to install.
