Vector Hexagon is live — our new twitch survival game

One cursor, a pulsing core, and walls closing in from every side — each leaving exactly one gap. Vector Hexagon, our Super Hexagon-inspired reflex game, is out now and playable free in your browser.

Vector Hexagon is a pure twitch survival game. Your cursor orbits a geometric core while walls sweep inward from the edges of the screen. Every wave leaves a single gap — rotate into it or the run ends. The playing field itself fights back: it morphs between square, pentagon and hexagon layouts (4, 5 and 6 lanes), spinning and pulsing as you survive. Runs are measured in seconds, restarts are instant, and "one more try" does the rest.

Climb the tiers — or go endless

The main mode is a three-tier climb. Survive 60 seconds on Hexagon and the game automatically advances you to Hexagoner, then Hexagonest — each tier faster and meaner than the last. Clear the final tier and you've beaten the game. Prefer a pure high-score chase? Endless mode keeps the walls coming for as long as you can keep dodging.

Signature walls with names

Alongside procedural waves, Vector Hexagon throws authored set-pieces at you — signature patterns with their names flashed on screen as they arrive: Spiral Staircase, Pendulum, The Bait, Twin Helix, The Pinch and Ladder Reversal. Learn to read them and your survival times jump.

Trophies and a global leaderboard

  • 31 trophies — 15 bronze, 10 silver, 5 gold and 1 platinum, from your first dodge to full mastery.
  • Two-tab high scores — your local bests plus a global leaderboard for every tier and for endless mode, with your world rank.
  • Cloud sync — sign in free and your trophies and scores follow you across devices.

Made for keyboard and thumb

On desktop, steer with the arrow keys or A/D. On mobile, hold the left or right half of the screen to rotate — the whole game is designed for one-thumb play, with fullscreen support and a mute setting that remembers your choice. Like every GameVolt game there's nothing to download, no ads and no signup required.
