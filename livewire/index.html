Livewire — Connect the Dots Without Crossing

Play Livewire free in your browser — a relaxing line-drawing puzzle where you connect every pair of same-colored dots with glowing wires. There's just one rule: wires can't cross each other or brush against another dot. Draw your lines cleanly and the board lights up. With 44 hand-tuned campaign levels, a date-seeded daily puzzle, an endless mode and 31 trophies to unlock, it's an easy game to pick up and a hard one to put down. No download, no signup — it runs instantly on desktop and mobile.

How to Play Livewire

Every board scatters pairs of colored dots inside a circular field. Press on a dot and drag to its matching partner to lay a wire. The wire follows your finger or mouse exactly, so the puzzle is all about routing: curve around obstacles, thread through gaps and leave room for the pairs still to come.

  • Drag from one dot to its same-colored partner to connect a pair.
  • Wires can't cross each other — a crossing is flagged with a red X until you fix it.
  • Wires can't touch other dots either, so plan your route carefully.
  • Tap a wire to remove it, use Undo to step back, or Clear to restart the board.
  • Stuck? The Hint button highlights a pair (or shows a ghost solution on hand-made levels) — but it costs a star.

Campaign, Daily Puzzle & Endless Mode

The campaign spans four difficulty packs — Easy, Normal, Hard and Expert — mixing hand-crafted levels with seeded generated boards that get tanglier as you go. Each level is timed and scored with up to three stars: solve it without a hint and without ever leaving a crossing on the board to earn a flawless three, and chase your personal best time on every level.

The daily challenge is generated from the date, so everyone in the world gets the same puzzle each day. Complete it to build your daily streak — 3-day and 7-day streaks unlock trophies. When you've cleared the campaign, endless mode serves up freshly generated boards that grow from three pairs toward six, tracking your best run — and every board you solve counts toward a global endless leaderboard when you sign in (free).

31 Trophies to Unlock

Livewire ships with 31 trophies across four tiers — 15 bronze, 10 silver, 5 gold and one platinum. They range from connecting your first pair to solving a level in under 15 seconds, earning three stars on every campaign level, or untangling a messy board back to clean. Unlock the other 30 and the platinum "Live Wire" trophy is yours. Progress is saved in your browser, and if you're signed in on GameVolt your trophies sync to your account.

Made for Touch

Livewire FAQ

Is Livewire free to play?

Yes — completely free in your browser, with no download and no signup. Progress is saved locally on your device, and signing in to GameVolt keeps your trophies synced.

What are the rules of Livewire?

Connect every pair of same-colored dots by dragging a wire between them. Wires may not cross each other and may not touch any other dot. When all pairs are connected and the board is conflict-free, the level is complete.

How do I earn three stars on a level?

Finish the level without using a hint and without ever having a crossing or dot-touch on the board. Using a hint costs one star, and letting a conflict appear costs another — careful routing beats fast scribbling.

Is the daily puzzle the same for everyone?

Yes. The daily challenge is generated from a date-based seed, so every player worldwide gets the identical board each day. Complete it daily to build your streak and unlock the streak trophies.

What happens if my wires cross?

The crossing is marked with a red X and the offending dots pulse red. You can't finish the level until it's resolved — tap a wire to remove it, redraw a better route, or use Undo to step back.

Does Livewire work on mobile?

Yes — it's designed for touch. Drag wires with your finger, and enjoy haptic feedback on devices that support it. The board automatically scales to fit your screen.
