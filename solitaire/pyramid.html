Play Pyramid Solitaire Online ‚Äì Free Card Game

Pyramid Solitaire is a unique card game where you remove pairs of cards that add up to 13. The cards are arranged in a pyramid shape, and your goal is to demolish the entire pyramid by finding matching pairs. Kings are worth 13 and can be removed on their own.

How to Play Pyramid

Select two exposed cards whose values total 13: Ace (1) + Queen (12), Two (2) + Jack (11), Three (3) + Ten (10), and so on. Kings equal 13 alone and are removed with a single click. Draw from the stock pile when no more matches are available. Clear the entire pyramid to win.

Tips & Strategy

Focus on removing cards near the top of the pyramid first to expose more options below. Pay attention to which cards are still hidden ‚Äì sometimes it's better to save a match for later if it will uncover more useful cards. Managing the stock pile wisely is key.
