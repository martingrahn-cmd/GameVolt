Spider Solitaire challenges you to build complete sequences of cards from King to Ace in the same suit. Once a full sequence is completed, it's removed from the tableau. Clear all cards to win. Choose from one, two, or four suits to match your skill level.

How to Play Spider Solitaire

Arrange cards on the tableau in descending order. You can move any descending sequence of the same suit as a group. When you run out of moves, deal a new row of cards from the stock. Complete all eight same-suit sequences from King down to Ace to win.
