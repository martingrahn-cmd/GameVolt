Free Solitaire Online – 6 Classic Card Games

Play Solitaire free in your browser with six beloved card games in one collection. Whether you're a Klondike purist, a FreeCell strategist or a Spider Solitaire fan, GameVolt has you covered – complete with global leaderboards to see how you stack up against players worldwide.

Six Games Included

Klondike – The classic solitaire everyone knows. Draw cards from the stock and build four foundation piles from Ace to King.

FreeCell – All cards are dealt face-up in this strategic variant. Use the four free cells wisely to move cards and clear the tableau.

Spider Solitaire – Build descending sequences of the same suit across the tableau. Available in one-suit, two-suit and four-suit difficulty.

Pyramid – Remove pairs of cards that add up to 13 from the pyramid-shaped layout. A satisfying puzzle-style solitaire game.

TriPeaks – Clear three peaks of cards by selecting cards one higher or lower than the current card. Fast-paced and great for quick sessions.

Golf Solitaire – Remove all cards from the tableau by playing cards one rank higher or lower. Simple rules, surprisingly deep strategy.
